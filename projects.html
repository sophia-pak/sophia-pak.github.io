Italian Mafia: An Introduction through PowerPoint

This presentation dives into the origins, evolution, and impact of the Italian Mafia — a powerful and secretive network that shaped both Italy and the world. Designed for those unfamiliar with the topic, it unravels how groups like the Cosa Nostra, Camorra, and ‘Ndrangheta emerged from Sicily’s social struggles, evolving into global criminal forces. From their deep cultural roots to their infamous code of silence, this presentation breaks down the Mafia’s history, structure, and influence (especially in media and literature) in an accessible and engaging way.
